MODE OF ADMISSION

One needs to take the JEE (Main) exam and clear it then give JEE Advanced exam

One also needs to secure at least 75% marks (65% for OBC-NCL, 65% for Defence, 50% for SC/ST/PwD) in aggregate of Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics in Class XII.

Admissions are done on the basis of rank in JEE (Advanced) through JOSAA Counselling.
